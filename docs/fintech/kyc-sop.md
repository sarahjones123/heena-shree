# KYC Compliance Workflow

## Objective

Standardize identity verification in compliance with regulatory requirements.

---

## KYC Steps

1. Collect identity proof.
2. Validate document authenticity.
3. Perform AML screening.
4. Assign risk tier.
5. Record verification outcome.

---

## Risk Classification

| Risk Level | Criteria |
|------------|----------|
| Low | Domestic salaried individual |
| Medium | Business owner |
| High | Politically exposed person |
